One the Sultan of Bijapur came to know about the strength of Kishnadevaraya's army. He also understood that Rayar was planningto attack him. The Sultan decided to stop it.

He knew the king was strong believer in astrology. He met his royal astrologer and requested him to stop the war and for this he would give the astrologer a large amount of money.

The greedy astrologer came to Vijayanagar palace. He understood that Krishnadevaraya was ready to attack Bijapur.

He thought to stop this. He went straight to the Maharaj. He presented s if he was hesitating to reveal something bad to the king. Then he said, “Maharaja, your planetary positions now not favourable. So I advise you not to make any attempt for a war.

The ministers and officers and the army chief were also of the same opinion because they were fond of the king's welfare. The king thought for a long time.

Tenali Raman was watching all these. He had a doubt about the astrologer. So he said to the king, “Why are you hesitating, oh maharaja? The Words of the astrologer never comes true always.”

The king accepted this and said, “You are correct Tenali Raman but how to convince to others? Do one thing. Announce that the one who proves that 1 the astrologer is wrong and he will get an award of thousand rupees.”

Tenali Raman was happy. He said, “Mharaja, I myself will prove this. Please give me the rights to punish the astrologer if he is wrong.” The king accepted.

Next day in the Royal Hall, the astrologer and Tenali Raman were sitting in the opposite sides . Raman was watching the astrologer very carefully. Then he asked him, “Do you believe what you predict will surely happen?”

“Why do you doubt it?” asked he astrologer.”

Raman said politely, “You may live for many more years and deliver forecasts to many people.”

The astrologer nodded his head. Raman asked, “Oh, then you will surely live some forty years more?”

“No doubt” said the astrologer.

“But your words are gone wrong” said Raman and drew the sword of the chief guard and cut off the head of the astrologer.

Everybody was taken aback. Raman took the palm leaf bundle of the astrologer and there was hidden in between the palm leaves a secret letter from the king of Bijapur sent to the astrologer which proved that the astrologer was a secret agent of the king of Bijapur.

The king praised Tenali Raman. He gave him ten thousand rupees as a reward for saving his life. Later he went on war against the king of Bijapur and conquered the kingdom.
