Once there was a huge music competition arranged at the Akbar's Court. Many talented musicians from all part of the kingdom were present at the palace.

A bull was brought to the court. The King announced that whoever makes the bull move and get7s the bull's interest by music will be the winner.

The competition started.

The musicians one by one, started to play their masterpieces but, the bull was standing still and paid no attention.

Finally, Birbal came to the stage. His music was so strange and sounded like droning of mosquitoes and mooing of cows. Everybody felt funny and started to laugh. But, to everyone's surprises the bull started to move and dance to his music.

Birbal was declared as the winner.
