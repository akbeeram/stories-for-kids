One day the Emperor asked Birbal, "Birbal, is it possible to be the both "noblest" and the "lowest" together?" Birbal said, "Yes, Jahaanpanaah" "Then bring me such a person."

Birbal went and returned next day with a beggar and presenting him to Akbar said, "This is the lowest among all of your subjects, Jahaanpanaah". Akbar asked, "Good, that......

"He has been given the honour of having an audience with the Emperor, that makes him the noblest among the beggars, Jahaanpanaah."
